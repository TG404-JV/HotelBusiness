Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes, all plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required.

Do I need special hardware?

No, TableFlow runs on standard Android phones, tablets, and any modern web browser. You don't need expensive proprietary hardware.

Can I upgrade or downgrade?

Absolutely. You can change your plan at any time directly from the dashboard.

Do you take a cut of my sales?

Never. We only charge the flat monthly subscription fee regardless of your volume.

How hard is the setup?

Most restaurants are fully set up in under 2 hours. Enterprise setups may take slightly longer for custom integrations.

Is offline mode supported?

The Waiter app can temporarily queue orders if Wi-Fi drops, and will sync securely once reconnected.
