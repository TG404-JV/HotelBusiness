Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

At TableFlow ("we," "our," or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our software applications.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and billing information when you register for an account.
  • Business Data: Information about your restaurant operations necessary to provide our service.
  • Usage Data: Information automatically collected when using our applications, such as IP addresses, browser types, and usage patterns.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our services
  • Process your transactions and manage your account
  • Improve our software and develop new features
  • Send administrative emails, technical notices, and security alerts
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. All sensitive/credit information you supply is transmitted via Secure Socket Layer (SSL) technology and then encrypted into our Payment gateway providers database.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@tableflow.com.
